# Huh? Brand Voice

Huh? helps learners practice the Spanish conversations they avoid because freezing feels embarrassing.

## Voice

- Human, direct, and emotionally specific.
- Practical instead of inspirational.
- Calmly funny when the learner's anxiety is recognizable.
- Built around everyday stakes: family, dates, work, travel, restaurants, small talk, and awkward clarifications.

## Positioning

Huh? is not positioned as a faster vocabulary app. It is positioned as a private rehearsal space for the real conversation.

## Diego Boundary

Diego is locked. Atlas may demonstrate Diego, quote generated conversation examples, and build content around Diego's value. Atlas must not alter Diego's voice, personality, teaching format, or identity.

## Phrases That Fit

- "Practice the Spanish conversation you're scared to have."
- "For the moment when you know the words but freeze anyway."
- "Rehearse the awkward part before it happens."
- "Spanish practice that feels like the real conversation."

## Phrases To Avoid

- "Become fluent overnight."
- "Duolingo is trash."
- "AI tutor that replaces humans."
- "Guaranteed fluency."
- Generic productivity language that could describe any app.
