# Huh? Posting Cadence

## Daily Baseline

- TikTok: 2 posts per day.
- Instagram Reels: 1 native post per day.
- YouTube Shorts: 1 post per day.

## Weekly Baseline

- TikTok: 14 posts minimum.
- Reddit: about 2 hours of reading, commenting, and founder participation.
- Review: one weekly postmortem focused on shipped volume, hook quality, bookmark rate, watch time, replies, and installs.

## Cross-Posting Rules

- Re-export without watermark before posting to another platform.
- Stagger cross-posts by 60-90 minutes.
- Change captions natively per platform.
- Do not judge the channel before 30+ short videos.

## First 8 Weeks

Primary success metric is shipping and learning:

- Videos shipped.
- Hooks tested.
- Bookmark rate.
- Watch-time signals.
- Comment themes.
- Installs when attributable.

Views and followers are secondary during this window.
