# Huh? Content Pillars

## 1. Freeze Moments

Short videos built around the painful second when the learner knows words but cannot speak.

Examples:

- Ordering food and blanking after the first sentence.
- Meeting a partner's family.
- Asking someone to repeat themselves.
- Answering a simple question too slowly.

## 2. Awkward Rehearsals

Diego helps the learner practice the uncomfortable but realistic conversation.

Examples:

- "How do I say I only understood half of that?"
- "How do I tell my suegra I liked dinner?"
- "How do I ask if I can pay separately?"

## 3. Gentle Corrections

Show Diego correcting naturally without shaming the learner.

Examples:

- Word choice correction.
- Gender/article correction.
- More natural phrasing.
- Tone adjustment for politeness.

## 4. Emotional Payoff

Close with relief, confidence, or recognition.

Examples:

- "That is exactly the sentence I needed."
- "I would actually say this."
- "Now I know what to practice before dinner."

## 5. App Clarity

The viewer should understand that Huh? is an iOS app and that Diego is the interaction surface.

Required:

- Show the real app or App Store screenshot.
- Make the next action obvious.
- Keep the value tied to one specific conversation.
