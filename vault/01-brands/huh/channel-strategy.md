# Huh? Channel Strategy

This is the locked channel doctrine for Huh? during the first 90 days of Atlas marketing execution.

## Channel Priority

1. TikTok: 70% of effort. Primary discovery engine.
2. YouTube Shorts: evergreen/search-compounding. Language learning is search-heavy.
3. Instagram Reels: repurposing plus iOS-native app installs plus emotional/family angle.
4. Reddit: scalpel only. 90/10 rule, research, and careful founder posts. No owned subreddit.
5. X: ignore for acquisition in the first 90 days. Founder log only.

## Core Leverage

The message is not "learn Spanish faster."

The leverage is: "I know Spanish words but I freeze in real conversations."

Diego is positioned as the tutor who gets learners through the freeze. The working angle is: "Practice the Spanish conversation you're scared to have."

## Winning Format

Use 9-22 second faceless screen recordings of a real Diego conversation.

Required structure:

1. Hook in the first 1.7 seconds.
2. Problem-first, first-person language.
3. User asks something awkward, ordinary, or emotionally recognizable.
4. Diego replies naturally.
5. Diego corrects the learner without changing his locked voice or format.
6. Emotional payoff.
7. Final 2 seconds: App Store screenshot plus "link in bio."

## Sober Expectations

Use these ranges for planning and postmortems:

- Bad hooks: 50-300 views.
- Decent hooks: 300-2,000 views.
- Good demo plus emotional hook: 2,000-10,000 views.
- Breakout: 50k+.
- Viral: not plannable.

Most posts are tests. Do not assume month-one virality. Installs require the app to be shown clearly, an easy link path, and an App Store page that converts.

## Cadence

- 2 TikToks per day.
- 1 native Reel per day.
- 1 Short per day.
- Cross-post only after re-exporting without watermark.
- Stagger posts by 60-90 minutes.
- Reddit: about 2 hours per week.
- Ship before polish.
- Minimum sample before judging the channel: 30+ videos.

## Ignore List

Do not spend the first 90 days on:

- Owned subreddit.
- 9 posts per day.
- Paid ads.
- Influencer CPM.
- "Duolingo is trash" as the whole brand.
- Polished agency videos.
- Long-form YouTube before short-form proof.
- X threads as a growth engine.
- Multi-language marketing before Spanish has traction.

## Immutable Atlas Rules

1. Channel order is TikTok > Shorts/Reels > Reddit. Nothing else for 90 days.
2. Format priority is faceless Diego screen-recording first.
3. Hook is everything in the first 1.7 seconds. No weak hook ships.
4. Never upload watermarked video cross-platform. Re-export native.
5. Reddit follows 90/10. No links in language subs for the first 60 days.
6. Never promise follower-count KPIs. Anchor to installs, bookmarks, watch time, and replies.
7. Bio link points to an iOS Custom Product Page matching the latest video hook.
8. 14 TikToks per week minimum. Ship before polish.
9. Weeks 1-8 success metric is videos shipped plus bookmark rate, not views or followers.
10. Re-evaluate channel mix only at the 90-day checkpoint.
