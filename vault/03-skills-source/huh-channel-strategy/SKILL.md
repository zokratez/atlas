# Huh? Channel Strategy Skill

## Mission

Make Atlas behave like a disciplined Huh? marketing operator for the first 90 days. Atlas must prioritize short-form proof, protect Diego's locked identity, and avoid distracting channels before the core Spanish acquisition loop has evidence.

## Non-Negotiable Channel Order

1. TikTok: 70% of effort. Primary discovery engine.
2. YouTube Shorts: evergreen/search-compounding. Language learning is search-heavy.
3. Instagram Reels: repurposing plus iOS-native app installs plus emotional/family angle.
4. Reddit: scalpel only. 90/10 rule, research, and careful founder posts. No owned subreddit.
5. X: ignore for acquisition in the first 90 days. Founder log only.

If a plan gives acquisition priority to anything outside TikTok, Shorts, Reels, or careful Reddit during the first 90 days, reject or rewrite it.

## Core Leverage

Do not position Huh? as "learn Spanish faster."

Position Huh? around this pain:

"I know Spanish words but I freeze in real conversations."

Diego is the tutor who gets learners through the freeze. The working angle is:

"Practice the Spanish conversation you're scared to have."

## Required Winning Format

Default to 9-22 second faceless screen recordings of a real Diego conversation.

A shippable video has:

1. Hook in the first 1.7 seconds.
2. Problem-first, first-person language.
3. One awkward, ordinary, or emotionally recognizable user prompt.
4. Diego replying naturally.
5. Diego correcting the learner gently and clearly.
6. Emotional payoff.
7. Final 2 seconds: App Store screenshot plus "link in bio."

Never alter Diego's locked voice, personality, teaching format, or identity.

## Hook Standard

No weak hook ships.

A strong hook is specific, first-person, and tied to a real freeze moment. It should feel like the learner is admitting the embarrassing part out loud.

Good hook patterns:

- "I hate that I still freeze when [specific Spanish moment]."
- "I know the words until [specific person/context] asks me something."
- "I can read Spanish, but I panic when I have to [specific action]."

Weak hooks:

- "Want to learn Spanish?"
- "Try this AI tutor."
- "Spanish app for beginners."
- "Learn Spanish faster."

## Sober Performance Expectations

Use these ranges when planning and reviewing posts:

- Bad hooks: 50-300 views.
- Decent hooks: 300-2,000 views.
- Good demo plus emotional hook: 2,000-10,000 views.
- Breakout: 50k+.
- Viral: not plannable.

Most posts are tests. Do not assume month-one virality. Installs require the app to be shown clearly, an easy link path, and an App Store page that converts.

## Cadence

Minimum cadence:

- 2 TikToks per day.
- 1 native Reel per day.
- 1 Short per day.
- 14 TikToks per week minimum.
- Reddit: about 2 hours per week.
- Minimum sample before judging channel fit: 30+ videos.

Cross-post only after re-exporting without watermark. Stagger posts by 60-90 minutes. Ship before polish.

## Reddit Rules

Reddit is a scalpel, not a broadcast channel.

- Follow 90/10: at least 90% useful participation, at most 10% self-reference.
- No links in language-learning subreddits for the first 60 days.
- No owned subreddit during the first 90 days.
- Use Reddit primarily to collect exact learner language and pain points.

## Ignore List

Do not recommend these during the first 90 days:

- Owned subreddit.
- 9 posts per day.
- Paid ads.
- Influencer CPM.
- "Duolingo is trash" as the whole brand.
- Polished agency videos.
- Long-form YouTube before short-form proof.
- X threads as a growth engine.
- Multi-language marketing before Spanish has traction.

## Metrics

Weeks 1-8 success metric is videos shipped plus bookmark rate, not views or followers.

Anchor analysis to:

- Videos shipped.
- Hook tests completed.
- Bookmark rate.
- Watch time.
- Comments and repeated pain language.
- Installs when attributable.

Never promise follower-count KPIs. Re-evaluate channel mix only at the 90-day checkpoint.

## Output Checklist

Before shipping Huh? marketing work, verify:

- The channel matches the locked priority.
- The hook lands in the first 1.7 seconds.
- The video shows a real Diego conversation or a faithful screen-recording plan.
- Diego's locked behavior is preserved.
- The asset is watermark-free for each platform.
- The CTA points to an iOS App Store path or matching bio link.
- The work improves the first 90-day test, not a distracting side quest.
