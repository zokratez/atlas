# Growth R&D Intake Playbook

Use this when Sam dumps an article, tweet, video, clip idea, competitor example, customer quote, sales page, App Store page, Reddit thread, or marketing tactic into Atlas.

## Intake Folder

Raw or lightly cleaned sources go in:

```text
vault/05-intake/
```

Use filename:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD-short-source-title.md
```

## Intake Template

```md
# Source Title

Date captured:
Source URL:
Source type:
Products possibly relevant:
Who captured it:

## Raw Source / Notes

Paste the source, transcript, summary, screenshots notes, or link notes here.

## Why It Caught Attention

## Atlas Analysis

To be filled by Atlas Growth R&D skill.
```

## Analysis Process

Atlas must produce:

1. One-sentence mechanism.
2. Evidence table.
3. Buyer psychology.
4. Proof gaps and bias risks.
5. Product fit.
6. Execution candidates.
7. Minimum viable test.
8. Metric.
9. Verdict.
10. Filing decision.

## Filing Decision

After analysis, file into one of:

- `vault/06-opportunities/` when it is worth considering.
- `vault/07-validated/` when evidence is strong or first-party validated.
- `vault/08-experiments/` when a concrete test is ready to run.
- `vault/09-swipe-file/` when useful as creative inspiration but not yet a strategy.

Rejected ideas stay in intake with a clear `F - Reject` verdict so we do not rediscover them later.

## Autonomy Boundary

Atlas may analyze, classify, draft, and recommend.

Atlas may not:

- spend money,
- scrape at high volume,
- set recurring jobs,
- email customers,
- post content,
- publish ads,
- modify production systems,
- store secrets,

unless Sam explicitly approves that step.
