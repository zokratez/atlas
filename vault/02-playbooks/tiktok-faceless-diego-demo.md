# TikTok Faceless Diego Demo Playbook

## Purpose

Create fast, repeatable short videos showing a real Diego conversation that resolves a specific Spanish freeze moment.

## Format

- Length: 9-22 seconds.
- Visual: screen recording of the Huh? app conversation.
- Hook: first 1.7 seconds, problem-first, first-person.
- Middle: learner asks one realistic question, Diego answers and corrects.
- End: emotional payoff plus App Store screenshot and "link in bio."

## Hook Formula

"I hate that I still freeze when [specific real-world Spanish moment]."

Examples:

- "I hate that I still freeze when my waiter asks one follow-up question."
- "I hate that I know Spanish until someone's mom asks me something."
- "I hate that I can read Spanish but panic when I need to answer."

## Production Checklist

- One conversation only.
- One pain point only.
- Diego response is visible long enough to read.
- No watermark on exported video.
- Caption matches the same freeze moment.
- Bio/App Store path matches the promise in the hook.
